Behavior:

	1. The user enters a temperature and chooses whether to convert it to Celsius or Fahrenheit.
	2. Upon clicking the submit button, the converted temperature is displayed on the page with the correct degree symbol (°C or °F).
	3. If no unit is selected, the user is prompted to choose one before converting.


This tool offers a simple and interactive way for users to convert temperatures between the two common units of measurement.
